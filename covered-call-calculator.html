What is a Covered Call?

A Covered Call is an options strategy where you own shares of a stock and sell call options against those shares. It generates income from the premium received, while capping upside potential.

Covered Call Payoff Structure

You hold 100 shares of stock and sell one call option at a strike price above the current price. Max profit is the premium plus any gain up to the strike. Max loss is the stock cost minus the premium. Breakeven is stock purchase price minus premium received.

📈 Max Profit

Strike price minus stock purchase price, plus premium received.

📉 Max Loss

Stock purchase price minus premium received.

⚖️ Breakeven

Stock purchase price minus the premium received.

🎯 Best For

Neutral to slightly bullish outlook. Generate income from existing stock positions.
